About

I am a software developer, web developer and writer from Olympia, Washington.

While coding and writing may seem worlds apart to some, I fell in love with the creativity of both from a young age. I've been writing creatively since I was 9 and coding since I was 14. In 2015, I published my first novel at 17 years old. I then went on to graduate summa cum laude from Carthage College, where I majored in English with a concentration in creative writing and minored in Computer Science. In May 2020, I will graduate from University of Washington's Web Developement Boot Camp.

When I am not coding or writing, I like to travel, read, and be with friends.
